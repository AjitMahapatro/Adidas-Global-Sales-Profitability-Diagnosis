# US Retail Revenue Intelligence Suite

A high-fidelity, two-page **Power BI** suite designed to analyze **$899.9M** in US retail revenue. This project focuses on identifying regional profit leakage and optimizing product mix through interactive executive tools.

## üìä Dashboard Overview

### 1. Executive Dashboard
![Executive Dashboard](Executive%20Dashboard)

The primary interface provides a high-level overview of organizational health and spatial performance.

* **KPI Tracking:** Monitors **Total Sales ($899.9M)**, **Operating Margin (36.91%)**, **Units Sold (2M)**, and **Operating Profit ($332.13M)**.
* **AI Root Cause Analysis:** Utilizes a **Decomposition Tree** to drill down from total sales into specific Regions, Retailers, and Products.
* **Spatial Analytics:** A **Bubble Map** visualizes market density across US cities to identify regional clusters.
* **Trend Analysis:** Tracks sales growth from **2020 to 2021** segmented by Sales Method (In-store, Online, Outlet).

### 2. Item Sales Analysis
![Item Sales Analysis](Item%20Sales%20Analysis)

This page focuses on granular product performance and merchandising efficiency.

* **Profitability Metrics:** Tracks a **Profit Per Unit** of **$133.99**.
* **Performance Matrix:** A scatter plot correlates **Total Sales** and **Operating Margin** by product category.
* **Inventory Insights:** Bar charts compare **Price per Unit** against **Units Sold** for categories like Men's Apparel and Women's Athletic Footwear.
* **Regional Mix:** A **Treemap** visualizes the distribution of product sales across different US regions.
